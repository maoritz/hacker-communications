Hello

Welcome to Hacker Communications! We specialize in all aspects of PR and communications. With us, the possibilities are endless. There are so many ways of effectively getting your brand out there. Based on your specific needs, we are here to help!

What we do

- Social Media -

Social media is playing an increasingly important role for business owners as they continue to look for ways to increase and protect their brand profile while deepening their connection with customers. Growing your business with social media can be overwhelming. We are here to simplify it for you, help you create engaging content, and target the right people.

- Digital Marketing -

Digital marketing is a very broad term, and we are here to help you with all of it! If you are looking to update or create a new website, we can help! We can also help with email marketing, social media advertising, and influencer marketing. There are many effective ways to get your brand recognized on the internet and to your consumers.

- Media Relations -

Boost your brand visibility with media coverage and social media partnerships. We will identify and target media and bloggers on all key platforms (print, digital, radio, tv) with creative, thought-provoking press material, including traditional and social press releases, media kits, etc. Media events are also an effective way to grow your brand’s presence online and in-person.
